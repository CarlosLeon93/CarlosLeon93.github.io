WHAT DO WE DO?

We do our best to give all of our doggies not just a “bathroom break”, but fun walks, a bunch of sniffing time and lots and lots of love (and of course treats ;) ). We form groups where dogs work well together, they like each other, become friends over time and so they look forward to meeting their buddies every day. We like to have relatively small groups (maximum 6 dogs in one group), we believe this way each of them are getting all the attention and care they need.
